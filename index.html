Global Community

I don't have any contact with the men on the right. My community is Astro JS. Astro JS is lot like a static site generator like Next.js, Gatsby JS, Eleventy, etc. It's based on the new Islands Architecture. It supports a total of six UI frameworks, React, Preact, Svelte, Vue, Lit & Solid JS. It also comes with it's own Astro component syntax. By default, Astro ships Zero kbs of JavaScript!
